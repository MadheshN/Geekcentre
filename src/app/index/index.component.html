Featured Comics

IRON MAN | 2019

Reality is in the eye of the beholder, as Tony Stark questions his humanity... while Jocasta is making the choice to leave her robotic body behind and upgrade to biological parts.

CAPTAIN AMERICA COMICS | 1941

Captain America and Bucky must battle a group of ageless monsters from the East who cannot be killed!

KING THOR | 2019

Thor of the far future—All-Father of a broken realm and a dying universe—as he stood in battle against Gorr, the Butcher of Gods, wielder of All-Black the Necrosword.

BATMAN WHO LAUGHS | 2019

Ripped from Batman’s greatest nightmares, the Grim Knight is his world’s most dangerous vigilante, unafraid to use any weapon and go to any lengths to stop those whom he deems worthy of death. Trained with the finest arsenal Wayne money can buy, learn the secret origin of the second-deadliest Batman, hand selected by the Batman Who Laughs to bring his dark plans to fruition.

SUPERMAN | 2005

FLASHPOINT | 1999

Featured Mangas

ONE PIECE

ONE PIECE is a legendary high-seas quest unlike any other. Luffy is a young adventurer who has longed for a life of freedom ever since he can remember. He sets off from his small village on a perilous journey to find the legendary fabled treasure, ONE PIECE, to become King of the Pirates

NARUTO

Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is told in two parts—the first set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens

BLEACH

Ichigo Kurosaki is a teenager from Karakura Town who can see ghosts, a talent allowing him to meet a supernatural human Rukia Kuchiki, who enters the town in search of a Hollow, a kind of monstrous lost soul who can harm both ghosts and humans.

BERSERK

Guts, a mercenary warrior drawn into the world of the God Hand, powerful beings who rule the supernatural realm. The story is set in the Kingdom of Midland, a fictional country inspired by medieval Europe

VAGABOND

Growing up in 17th century Sengoku era Japan, Shinmen Takezou is shunned by the local villagers as a devil child due to his wild and violent nature. Running away from home with a fellow boy at age 17, Takezou joins the Toyotomi army to fight the Tokugawa clan at the battle of Sekigahara.

PARASYTE

Shinichi Izumi, a high school senior who is the victim of a failed attempt by a parasyte organism to take over his brain. Instead of burrowing into his brain, the parasyte landed in his hand and matured partially up his arm.
